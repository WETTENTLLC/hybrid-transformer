Technical Specifications

Our High-Efficiency Transformers are engineered to meet or exceed industry standards for energy performance. Below are typical specifications for our HET series. Custom configurations are available.

Specification Details (Example Model: HET750-13.8) Notes
Power Rating 750 kVA Range: 50 kVA - 5000 kVA
Primary Voltage 13.8kV Δ Customizable (e.g., 480V, 4160V, 34.5kV)
Secondary Voltage 480Y/277V Customizable (e.g., 208Y/120V, 600V)
Frequency 60 Hz 50 Hz models available
Efficiency (DOE 2016) > 99.3% at 50% load Exceeds NEMA Premium / DOE standards
No-Load Losses < 0.15% of rated power Optimized core design
Load Losses (at 75°C) < 0.8% of rated power Low resistance windings
Impedance 5.75% Customizable (e.g., 4-8%)
Cooling Type ONAN / AN (Dry Type) or ONAN (Liquid) Options: ONAF, KNAN, VPI/Cast Resin (Dry)
Winding Material Copper (Cu) / Aluminum (Al) Based on design and customer preference
Core Material Amorphous Metal / Laser-Scribed GOES Ultra-low loss materials
Sound Level NEMA ST-20 compliant Reduced noise designs available
Temperature Rise 150°C (Dry) / 65°C (Liquid) Other temperature rises available
Enclosure Type NEMA 1 (Indoor) / NEMA 3R (Outdoor) Custom enclosures available
Applicable Standards DOE 2016, IEEE C57.12.00/01, IEC 60076 UL Listed / CSA Certified options
