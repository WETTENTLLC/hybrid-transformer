About AMC Hybrid Transformers

Pioneering the Future of Power Quality

AMC Hybrid Transformers is a leader in power quality and harmonics, bringing over 20 years of accumulated know-how to the field. Our focus is on providing a one-stop service that covers Harmonic Diagnosis, Solution Provision, and Maintenance for our flagship Hybrid Transformer.

The Hybrid Transformer is a testament to our pioneering spirit. It is an all-in-one solution that ingeniously combines transformer functionality, harmonic attenuation, and unbalanced improvement. This groundbreaking technology was developed through collaborative R&D and an integrated approach that reduces both investment and maintenance costs by eliminating the need for separate harmonic filters.

Widespread Adoption and Trusted Performance

Our Hybrid Transformer is an industry-first development that has been supplied to hundreds of major institutions worldwide. The high repurchase rate underscores the satisfaction and trust customers have in this technology. Our transformers are widely adopted by major organizations across diverse sectors including airports, power plants, utilities, and large enterprises.

Trusted by major organizations across the globe.

Globally Recognized Technology

Our commitment to excellence is validated by leading technology certifications and patents for the Hybrid Transformer in Korea, the USA, and China.

Our Technology

AMC Hybrid Transformers utilize patented Zig-Zag Winding and advanced core materials to deliver industry-leading harmonic attenuation, energy efficiency, and reliability. Our R&D team continuously innovates to address the evolving needs of modern power systems.

  • Patented Zig-Zag Winding for harmonic reduction
  • Advanced core and insulation materials
  • Integrated monitoring and diagnostics
  • Compliance with IEC, IEEE, and global standards
