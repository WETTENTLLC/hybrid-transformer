About AMC Hybrid Transformers

Pioneering the Future of Power Quality

AMC Hybrid Transformers specializes in providing the first hybrid transformer to the USA, Canada, and Mexico.

Our transformers are a breakthrough technology that eliminate the need for harmonic filtering, while reducing heat production, energy loss, and lowering maintenance costs.

Our Products

We offer the new patented technology of the Enertech Hybrid Transformers. These are now available to the United States, Mexico, and Canada through our distributor partners.

They save enough energy and eliminate the cost of a harmonic filter, paying for themselves in 3.5 years in savings.

Our Commitment

We and our partners are committed to providing our clients with exceptional service. We stand for cleaner energy with less environmental impact. Our company was created to bring a new transformer technology to North America.

Introducing dramatic reductions in harmonic distortion without need of harmonic filter, while reducing heat and power loss. Saving both money and CO2 emissions.
