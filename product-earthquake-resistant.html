Enhanced Durability and Safety

The design of Enertech's Hybrid Transformers incorporates features for enhanced durability and safety, including robust seismic resistance. This ensures the transformer maintains structural integrity and operational reliability even during significant seismic events, making it a trusted solution for critical infrastructure in any location.

Key components contributing to this resilience include:

  • Support Frame & Top Frame: Provide a rigid superstructure.
  • Damper Bar & Friction Damper: These components are specifically designed to absorb and dissipate seismic energy, protecting the core and coils from damaging vibrations.
  • Reinforced Base & Bottom Frame: Ensure a secure and stable connection to the concrete foundation, preventing movement and toppling.
