Key Manufacturing Capabilities

  • Advanced Winding Technology: Precision-controlled winding machines for optimal coil geometry and performance.
  • Automated Core Cutting & Stacking: Ensuring minimal core losses and high efficiency through precise lamination processing.
  • VPI/VPE Impregnation Systems: State-of-the-art vacuum pressure impregnation for superior insulation and environmental protection in dry-type transformers.
  • Robotic Welding & Fabrication: Consistent, high-quality tank and enclosure fabrication.
  • ISO Class Clean Assembly Areas: For critical components requiring controlled environments.
  • Comprehensive Testing Suite: Including routine tests, type tests (e.g., impulse, short-circuit, temperature rise), and special tests as per customer requirements and international standards (IEC, ANSI/IEEE).
  • Lean Manufacturing Principles: Continuous improvement processes to enhance efficiency, reduce waste, and ensure quality.
  • Supply Chain Integration: Robust supplier quality management and just-in-time material flow.

Quality Control at Every Stage

Our ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system is embedded in every step of our manufacturing process. From incoming material inspection to in-process checks and final product testing, we maintain the highest standards. Each transformer undergoes a rigorous testing regime to verify its performance, safety, and reliability before shipment.

  • Incoming material inspection and verification.
  • In-process quality checks at critical assembly points.
  • Dimensional and visual inspections.
  • Electrical testing (resistance, ratio, polarity, phase relation, no-load loss, load loss, impedance, insulation resistance, dielectric tests).
  • Specialized type testing as required.
  • Final inspection and documentation review.
